Notes:
- tpd = (|cell_fall| + |cell_rise|) / 2
- B=0: TG2 passes ~A to Y (inverting path through A-inverter + TG2)
- Energy measured over one full transition cycle (1400 ps period)
- Power = energy * frequency (normalized to 1 GHz for comparison)
- EDP = energy * tpd (energy-delay product, lower is better)
- 0.40V SLVT achieves 1.36x energy reduction at 2.15x delay cost
- nfin=4 (2x drive) vs XOR2x1 nfin=2: faster transitions, ~same tpd structure
