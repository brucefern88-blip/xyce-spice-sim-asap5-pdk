Notes:
- tpd = (|cell_rise| + |cell_fall|) / 2
- Topology: XOR TG core drives internal node X (Cext=0.120fF), output INV buffers to Y
- B=0: TG2 passes ~A to X, inverter gives Y=A (non-inverting overall)
- Output inverter provides full-swing restoration and sharp transitions
- fall/rise_transition much smaller than XOR2x1 due to inverter output stage
- Energy measured over one full transition cycle (1400 ps period)
- 0.40V SLVT achieves 1.40x energy reduction at 2.78x delay cost
- Delay penalty vs XOR2x1: ~37% slower due to extra inverter stage + Cext_X load
- EDP worse at 0.40V (132.7 vs 66.9 aJ*ps) — voltage scaling less favorable for 2-stage
